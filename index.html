The glorious ginger cake has its origins in the highlands of scotland, where it roamed ferraly free alongside the noble haggis. It took centuries and cost countless lives to domesticate this fierce cake,as its fiery bite has set aflame many those who dared enter its domain. In modern times however, the common house ginger cake, or gingies as they are lovingly known, have become a widespread family pet renown for their loyalty and pleasant aroma.
